StepSystemAction
1.External Air sourceCONNECT RIGHT

3.External airflowON

a. Right Ignition ButtonPRESS AND HOLD

a. Right Ignition ButtonRELEASE

a. External airflowSTOP

c. Idle RPM65 plus minus 1%
d. Right Boost Pump30 plus minus 5 PSI

f. Hydraulic PressureWITHIN LIMITS
